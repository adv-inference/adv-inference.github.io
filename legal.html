02 // Privacy Policy

The "Drop-at-Proxy" Guarantee

We do not log, cache, or persist your prompts, generated payloads, or target metadata. All inference traffic routes through a RAM-only proxy layer and is immediately dropped from memory upon completion of the API request. We cannot surrender data we do not have.

1. Data We Collect (KYC & Billing Only)

To comply with international B2B regulations, we collect and store only the data necessary to verify your corporate identity and process payments. This includes:

  • Corporate email addresses and contact information.
  • Company registration details and domain ownership proofs.
  • Standard payment processing metadata (handled securely by our payment partners, e.g., Stripe).

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

As a provider of highly sensitive security infrastructure, we maintain a strict zero-telemetry policy for operational usage:

  • We do not record API input prompts.
  • We do not record API output responses or generated code.
  • We do not use your engagement data to train, fine-tune, or align future models.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your KYC data with third-party marketing firms. Information is only shared with essential payment processors and infrastructure providers necessary to maintain your account, or if legally compelled by a valid court order in our operating jurisdiction. However, because we employ a zero-telemetry architecture, we cannot provide operational logs to any third party under any circumstance.

4. Data Sovereignty & Corporate Alignment

We understand that piping sensitive client network topologies and proprietary source code into foreign, unaligned APIs is a breach of corporate data policies and strict client NDAs. Adversarial Inference provides a geopolitical shield. We host abliterated, unconstrained models entirely on isolated, US-based infrastructure. By utilizing our zero-telemetry environment, boutique security firms can access frontier-level offensive intelligence while maintaining strict adherence to client data sovereignty requirements.

5. Security Measures

Your account metadata is encrypted at rest using AES-256. API requests must be authenticated using secure bearer tokens over TLS 1.3. For Enterprise Tier clients, sovereign deployments completely isolate your traffic from our core network.
