Hard Truths // Operations

Direct answers for technical B2B stakeholders.

01/ I can download abliterated models for free. Why pay for API access?

Running 70B+ parameter models on a laptop during an engagement is a massive bottleneck. It drains battery, causes thermal throttling, and lacks the throughput required for parsing large logs. You aren't just paying for weights; you are paying for dedicated enterprise GPU compute, 99.9% uptime, and a specialized RAM-only proxy layer that guarantees zero telemetry for your payloads.

02/ What prevents threat actors from using your API?

Strict operational security. We do not offer anonymous sign-ups. Access requires manual B2B identity verification, corporate domain validation, and a verified proof-of-work history in the cybersecurity industry.

03/ Is my payload data used to train your models?

Absolutely not. Our infrastructure is built on a "Drop-at-Proxy" architecture. Prompts and responses exist only in volatile RAM during the inference lifecycle. No data is logged, cached, or persisted for training purposes.

04/ Where is your infrastructure physically located?

Our standard clusters are hosted in highly secure, Tier 4 data centers in neutral jurisdictions. For 'Enterprise Tier' clients, we offer sovereign deployment options to ensure data never crosses specific geographic borders.
